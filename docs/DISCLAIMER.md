# Disclaimer

AI1SAD estimates environmental and surveillance-relevant shark encounter conditions. It does not predict individual attacks or guarantee safety outcomes.

AI1SAD scores are not emergency alerts, beach-closure orders, safety guarantees, or individual risk predictions. They are deterministic research and planning signals based on available public, environmental, and contextual data.

Users should follow official guidance from lifeguards, beach managers, weather services, wildlife authorities, emergency services, and local governments.

AI1SAD must not be used to infer shark intent, assign blame, classify activity as automatic provocation, or publish private victim/source information.
