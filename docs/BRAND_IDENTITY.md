# AI1SAD Brand Identity

## Name

- Product name: `AI1SAD`
- Expanded name: `Adaptive Intelligence for Integrated Shark Activity Detection`

## Mission Statement

AI1SAD provides calm, explainable marine intelligence for environmental monitoring and operational surveillance planning across coastal zones.

## Canonical Artwork Source

The canonical source artwork location is `images/branding/`.

- Reddit avatar source: `images/branding/reddit-avatar-under-500kb.jpg`
- Primary avatar source: `images/branding/avatar-primary.png`
- Alternate avatar source: `images/branding/avatar-alt.jpeg`
- Reddit banner source: `images/branding/banner-reddit.jpg`
- GitHub/social banner source: `images/branding/banner-github.png`
- Brand concept board: `images/branding/branding_asset_collection.png`

`docs/assets/brand/` and `frontend/public/brand/` are deployment copies for MkDocs and frontend runtime surfaces.

## Visual Tone

- Marine intelligence
- Radar and sonar scanning
- Operational mapping
- Environmental monitoring
- Calm scientific dashboard

## Core Palette

- Deep Ocean: `#06263A`
- Slate Navy: `#1B2A41`
- Surveillance Teal: `#0F8B8D`
- Coastal Cyan: `#2EC4D6`
- Soft Ice: `#D9F0FF`
- Surveillance Purple: `#7B2CBF`

## Typography

- Dashboard and docs body: clean sans-serif for readability
- Headers: compact, high-contrast, operational style
- Labeling: short, precise, and non-sensational

## Logo Usage

- MkDocs deployment logo: [ai1sad-emblem.png](assets/brand/ai1sad-emblem.png)
- Frontend deployment logo: `frontend/public/brand/ai1sad-emblem.png`
- Use on white or Soft Ice backgrounds
- Maintain clear space equal to one logo ring width on all sides
- Do not stretch, rotate, recolor outside palette, or add glow effects

## What To Avoid

- Blood-red horror shark imagery
- Attack-prediction language
- Sensational warning graphics
- Militarized weapons aesthetic
- Cartoon mascot style
